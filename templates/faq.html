What are Chatterbots ?

A chatbot (or chatterbot, or chat bot) is a computer program designed to simulate an intelligent conversation with one or more human users via auditory or textual methods. Programs playing this role are sometimes referred to as Artificial Conversational Entities, talk bots or chatterboxes. (Wikipedia.org, 2015)

What is artificial intelligence (AI)?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the intelligence exhibited by machines or software and the branch of computer science that aims to create it. Textbooks define the field as “the study and design of intelligent agents,” where an intelligent agent is a system that perceives its environment and takes actions that maximize its chances of success. (Wikipedia.org, 2015)
